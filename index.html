Our Story

August, 2007


After finishing his B.Tech in Pune, he gets admitted to UCI for Masters. Looking forward to coming to US, he joins UCI Orkut community to get to know other people joining UCI too. There he meets her ... for the first time 

August, 2007


Done with her Masters in Mathematics from Delhi, she packs her bags to leave for US to attend UCI and while looking through the UCI Orkut community stumbles upon him. They exchange a few hi and hellos here and there 

September, 2007


They meet on the first day in UCI in orientation and are glad to see each other 

2008


In the next few months they get acquainted with each other. They keep on seeing each other on campus every now and then. During the year and half at UCI, this acquaintance soon grows into a strong bond of friendship 

February, 2009


He graduates from UCI and goes on to take a job with FICO in San Diego. They bid goodbye to each other with a promise to keep in touch and be friends forever

December, 2009


She graduates from UCI and is looking for a job. Lucky for him, she lands a job in FICO !

January, 2010


While in San Diego, they go on countless lunches and dinners together and get to know each other better

May, 2011


She moves to New York to work for Citi Bank. They part ways, hoping they survive the long distance and time difference

June, 2012


He moves out of San Diego to San Jose to work for Paypal. They have survived the long distance so far

February, 2013


She moves back to California to work for Fiserv in Sunnyvale. Yay ! Things are back to normal :)

October, 2013


They finally decide to take the next big step in their lives ! Hallelujah !

. . . and so on Dec 26th, they will be stepping into the next phase of their lives and embark upon a journey of togetherness. Along with the love and support of their families they would really appreciate if their friends and extended family would bless the occasion with their presence . . .
